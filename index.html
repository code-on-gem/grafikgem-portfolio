CLARIZZA GEM MIRALLES
Santa Rosa Based Multimedia Artist
Clarizza Gem Miralles, known as "GRAFIKGEM," is a graphic designer who's also specializing in tattoo design. Recognized for her dark, moody visuals and gritty textures, she draws inspiration from alternative music and vintage aesthetics to create intricate designs and music posters that reflect raw emotion. She is currently expanding her tattoo design portfolio with plans to start inking her work.
